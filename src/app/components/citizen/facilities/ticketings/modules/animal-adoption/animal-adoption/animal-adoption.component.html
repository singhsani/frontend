{{'Rules for adopting wildlife animal /birds (Friends of Zoo)'| translate: translateKey}}

  1. Animals/ Birds adopted will be under the sole ownership of the zoo. The adopter will be considered as a FRIEND of the animal/ bird that he/she has adopted.
  2. Total expenses towards maintenance of the adopted animals/birds have to be paid by cheque and a receipt of the same will be issued.
  3. The adopted animals/birds will be maintained as per the zoo facilities and norms. The adopter will not be permitted to touch the adopted animals/birds or even feed any outside tood.
  4. Person/organization/company adopting Lions / Tigers / Leopards will be permitted to put NAME PLATE on respective enclosures of these animal BUT as per size allowed by the corporation.
  5. The Friend of Zoo (Adopter) will be awarded a certificate from Vadodara Municipal Corporation and also 25 entry tickets free of cost.
  6. Adopting person/organization/company will be invited for any educational programmes organized in the zoo.
  7. The zoo welcomes contributions in the form of necessities for protection against cold, hot and rainy weathers.
  8. The zoo also welcomes contribution in the form of wheelchairs, sound system, water cooler benches, chair etc. which could be useful for visitors in the zoo.
  9. Contributions/donations are also accepted on Environment Day, World Animal Day, Makar Sankranti, Parushan and other such days and also in memory of their loved ones on birthdays and death aniversaries towards daily / monthly feed expense. Such contributions will be accepted by cheques against which receipt will be issued.
  10. Any objections raise regarding adoption will be taken up in the cours (under Baroda jurisdiction) only.
  11. Over and above rules mentioned above. Municipal Commissioner exercises the right to add new rules deemed fit. The Municipal Commissioner also exercises the right to decide whether an animal bird can given for adoption or not.
  12. In the event of the adopted animal/ bird being exchanged with another zoo or organization or death during adopting period, the Friends of the Zoo (adopter) will be not be paid back any amount. le ne
  13. Adoptions are also permitted on 6 months or half yearly basis person.
  14. Adoption fees will be a onetime payment.
