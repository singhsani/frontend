{{head_lines| translate: translateKey}}

Note : Planetarium tickets will be non-refundable

{{'rules_and_regulations'| translate: translateKey}}

  1. Citizens follow the VMC Rules.
  2. Production of all necessary permission is mandatory for applicant before commencement of Program.
  3. Planetarium Show timings:
    • Special Show: 1. 03:00 PM to 03:30 PM (Gujarati) 2. 03:00 PM to 03:30 PM (Hindi) only for school.
    • General Show 1. 04:00 PM to 04:30 PM (Gujarati) 2. 05:00 PM to 05:30 PM (Hindi) 3. 06:00 PM to 06:30 PM(Hindi).
  4. Charge:

    • Special show charge per seat 10 ({{rupeeSign}}), Only with school approval.

    • General show charge per seat 20 ({{rupeeSign}})

  5. Seating Capacity: For Special Show citizen can book 98 seats and for General Show one user can book 4 seats.
