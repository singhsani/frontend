{{'Terms and Conditions for Adoption of Zoo Animals/ Birds in Sri Sayajibaug Zoo, VMC under the ‘Friends of Zoo’ program.'| translate: translateKey}}
  1. Hon’ble Municipal Commissioner, Vadodara Municipal Corporation (VMC) reserves all the rights regarding the Adoption of Zoo Animals/Birds under the ‘Friends of Zoo’ program.
    2. Hon’ble Municipal Commissioner, VMC reserves the right to:
    i. decide which Zoo Animals/Birds may be offered for Adoption.
    ii. amend the Terms and Conditions for Adoption as and when required.
    iii. revise the maintenance expenses of the Zoo Animals/Birds offered for Adoption.
  2. The Zoo Animals/Birds offered for Adoption shall always remain under the sole ownership of Sri Sayajibaug Zoo, VMC. The generous Donor shall be considered only as a ‘FRIEND’ of the Zoo for the Animal/Bird which he/she has adopted. The Donor shall here onwards be referred to as ‘FRIEND’.
  3. The Zoo Animals/Birds offered for Adoption shall be maintained within the Zoo Facility only and the minimum standards of upkeep of Zoo Animals as per the Central Zoo Authority (CZA), New Delhi, guidelines will be followed.
  4. The FRIEND shall neither be permitted to have any physical contact with the Zoo Animal/Bird that he/she has adopted, nor the FRIEND will be allowed to bring any outside food for the Animal/Bird which he/she has adopted.
  5. The Zoo Authority shall display the FRIEND’s Name on a signage at the respective Enclosure of the Animal/Bird that the FRIEND has chosen for Adoption.
    Please Note: The FRIEND shall give the details of the Name only which he/she desires to be displayed on the signage. The dimensions and format of the signage is already fixed by the Zoo Authority.
  6. The Zoo Authority shall facilitate the FRIEND by permitting 25 Free Entries to the Zoo throughout his/her period of Adoption.
    Please Note: The Free Entry during the Adoption Period will be given only on a prior intimation by the FRIEND to the Zoo Office. For availing the Free Entry, the FRIEND is hereby requested to liaison with the Zoo Education Officer; Contact No.: +91 9033340778.
  7. The FRIEND shall be honoured by awarding a ‘Certificate of Appreciation’ duly signed by the higher Authorities of VMC.
  8. The FRIEND may be invited as a Guest for some of the Awareness/Educational programs organized by the Zoo.
  9. The FRIENDS are requested to take a kind note that the amount paid towards the Adoption Scheme is Non-Refundable. Therefore, in the event of death of the Animal/Bird under adoption during the adoption period, the amount will not be paid back.
  10. In addition to the Point No. 9, during the period of adoption the Animal/Bird under adoption may be exchanged with another Zoo and in this case also the amount will not be paid back.

    ❖ GENEROUS CONSIDERATIONS:


    I. In addition to the Animal Adoption Scheme of Zoo; the Zoo shall be highly obliged to receive contributions from Individuals/Corporates in the form of Commodity, which are a special seasonal requirements for Animals/Birds for their better upkeep viz. Coolers during Summer, Heaters during Winter, Water Troughs, Feed Baskets, etc.
    II. Donations are also accepted on the occasions of major Festivals; on the occasions of Birthdays and/or Death Anniversary; to commemorate days like World Environment Day, World Animal Day, International Day for Biodiversity; Wildlife Week, etc.
    III. Donations are also welcomed to strengthen the Public Amenities at the Zoo likewise Wheelchairs, RO Water Systems at Drinking Water Points, Benches at the Resting Points, Dust Bins, etc.
    * Corporate Organizations can include the above Considerations in their CSR activity towards Environment and Nature Conservation.
